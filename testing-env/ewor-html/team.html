Partners

Daniel Dippold

Co-Founder & CEO

Daniel has a strong background in mathematics, computer science, and entrepreneurship, with studies at Cambridge, ETH Zurich, and St. Gallen. He sold his first venture in his early twenties and has since built companies valued at over €200 million. As co-founder of New Now Group GmbH, he enables clients like Deutsche Bank, Postbank, Eon, Philips, and Wella to fine-tune algorithms post-deployment through their software. He also founded and scaled Sigma Squared Society e.V., now the world’s largest community of U26 entrepreneurs, spanning over 30 countries with 200 global directors. Many Sigma members have gone on to become unicorn founders. Additionally, Daniel has been an early investor in several unicorns and has invested in over 50 companies to date.

Florian Huber

Co-Founder & Chief Investment Officer

A Berlin-based founder, entrepreneur, and startup investor, Florian founded united-domains, acquired by the 1&1 Group in 2015, and neubau kompass, Germany's leading real estate marketplace for new residential developments. As an early-stage investor, he holds stakes in 50 tech startups. In 2018, he was named 'Business Angel of the Year' by the German Business Angels Association. Additionally, Florian served as a General Partner at Signature Ventures, a VC fund focused on investing in web3 startups.

Alexander Grots

Co-Founder & CCO

Alex co-founded ProGlove, which sold for €500M, and has served as Managing Director at IDEO, where he played a key role in articulating the Design Thinking Method. Furthermore, he has initiated and led numerous innovation and entrepreneurship programmes at universities worldwide.

Paul H. Müller

Partner

A prominent angel investor, Paul is the co-founder of Adjust, a company that achieved a €1.2B exit and powers thousands of apps with built-in intelligence and automation, backed by responsive global customer support. In addition to his entrepreneurial success, he is also an avid race car enthusiast.

Petter Made

Partner

Petter Made is a fintech pioneer with over 25 years of experience. He co-founded SumUp, a global company valued at €8.5B, serving over 4 million SMBs across 36 countries. Then, as CPTO at Drooms, he led the development of the world’s fastest virtual data room, scaling the business to nine figures. Petter’s earlier career saw him in leadership roles at Inatec Payment AG, Ongame Networks, and Chinsay AB.

Quinten Selhorst

Partner

Quinten Selhorst was co-founder and former CEO of felyx, the market leader in shared e-mobility in the Benelux, which he scaled to millions in revenue, hundreds of employees, and operations across multiple countries. After exiting felyx, Quinten co-founded the Dutch Operator Fund, supporting Dutch startups in the pre-seed and seed stages. His extensive portfolio includes direct investments and limited partner roles in prominent early-stage funds like NP-Hard Ventures and Common Magic.
