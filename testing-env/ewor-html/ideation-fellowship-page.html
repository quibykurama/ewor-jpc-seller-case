More FAQs
What is the EWOR Ideation Fellowship?

For those who don’t have traction for their business idea yet, the EWOR Ideation Fellowship turns an aspiring entrepreneur into a successful tech entrepreneur.

Do I need a business idea to apply?

Ideally yes, but not necessarily. The EWOR Ideation Fellowship is designed for those who don’t have traction for their business idea yet. The EWOR Ideation Fellowship is entirely remote and turns aspiring entrepreneurs into business owners.

What is the difference between the Ideation and Traction Fellowship?

For those who don’t have traction for their business idea yet, the EWOR Ideation Fellowship turns an aspiring entrepreneur into a business owner. For those who do already have traction, the EWOR Traction Fellowship is designed to help them scale their venture. The Traction Fellowship is split into two phases to make it the most effective. As founders of some of the most successful companies in Europe, our lecturers and mentors have all learned first-hand what it takes to succeed in the business world. They give engaging live lectures in phase one, while the second phase gives EWOR members access to hundreds of resources on our platform, network, and feedback sessions with our mentors.

Where can I apply to the EWOR Ideation Fellowship?

Please use the application form on our website to apply for our Fellowships. It’s an extensive questionnaire that inquires about your experiences, motivations, and qualifications. Applicants typically take at least 30 minutes to complete the questionnaire.

Are you looking for talents to develop their ideas in a specific industry or technology sector?

We do not limit ourselves and our talent pool to a specific industry or technology sector.

How many talents do you accept?

Our selection committee consists of successful serial entrepreneurs and start-up investors - including several unicorn founders. The EWOR Fellowships have an acceptance rate of 0.1%.

What are the benefits of the EWOR Fellowships?

The EWOR Fellowships are your launchpad to unicorn status. As an aspiring high-growth entrepreneur, you'll gain access to a powerful ecosystem designed to accelerate your journey from idea to industry disruption. Our program offers intensive, hands-on learning from seasoned unicorn founders and industry titans who've been in your shoes and scaled to billion-dollar valuations.
‍
You'll dive deep into cutting-edge strategies for rapid scaling, securing VC funding, and building world-class teams. Our global network opens doors to potential co-founders, early adopters, and investors crucial for your startup's hypergrowth. Through real-world projects and personalized mentorship, you'll develop the visionary leadership and execution skills needed to transform industries.
‍
EWOR doesn't just teach entrepreneurship; it immerses you in it. You'll emerge with a battle-tested business model, a robust network, and the unshakeable confidence to take on incumbents and create the next billion-dollar company. If you're ready to think big, move fast, and leave a lasting impact on the world, the EWOR Fellowship is your first step towards unicorn success.

What time are the live sessions? Do I need to attend them?

The live sessions are in the late afternoon or early evening CET time, but you will receive information on your schedule during the onboarding process.

We do expect our Fellows to be present for these input and feedback sessions. However, we understand that in some cases, circumstances will keep talents from participating.

Are there any tuition fees for the Ideation Fellowship?

In place of formal tuition fees, EWOR takes 3% equity in companies that talents build by the end of the Ideation Fellowship. In addition, EWOR will invest up to €150K in selected start-ups at €1.0M post-money valuation cap.

Apart from the 3% that EWOR receives, are there any other shareholding privileges allocated to any third party?

Typically, there are no additional equity allocations to third parties.

Do you offer funding?

EWOR may invest up to €150k in selected start-ups and connect you to our investor network.

Please note that not all fellows receive investment.

What happens if I do not end up founding a company?

If you do not end up founding a company, EWOR will not require any equity from you.
